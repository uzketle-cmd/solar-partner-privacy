Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Solar Partner ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information in connection with the Solar Partner mobile and web applications and related services. We emphasise minimal data collection and privacy-by-design across the product.

Our Privacy Commitment

Solar Partner is engineered with privacy by design. We deliver professional solar analytics while minimising collection of personal data. We do not collect or retain email addresses, phone numbers, full physical addresses, or precise device location tracking by default.

Privacy by Default

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate and improve the app.

No Personal Identifiers
We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, financial data, or long-term precise GPS traces by default.
Local Storage First
Project data is stored locally on your device. Cloud backup is optional and enabled only when you opt in.
Anonymous Analytics
We only collect aggregated, anonymized usage statistics for performance and reliability improvements.
No Data Monetization
We do not sell, trade, or rent personal data. Our revenue is derived from product features and subscriptions.

Where needed, we call third-party services to provide core functionality. We limit shared data to what is strictly necessary and prefer ephemeral requests (not stored).

Service Type Purpose Data Shared Privacy Impact
Solar Data APIs (e.g. Open-Meteo) Provide radiation and weather data for analysis Coordinates for the requested computation (one-time, not persisted by us) Minimal; data used to compute results and discarded
Analytics (e.g. Firebase Analytics) Performance and usage metrics Aggregated, anonymized event data No personal identification; opt-out available per platform
Crash Reporting (e.g. Crashlytics) Debugging and stability reports Technical logs; we avoid including identifiable personal data Used to fix issues; retained briefly for debugging
Advertising N/A None We do not use behavioral advertising

Legal Disclosure

We may disclose information if required by law (e.g., subpoena, court order). Because we collect minimal personal data, such disclosures are expected to be extremely limited.

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All network traffic uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • Access Controls: Vendor access is limited and audited.
  • Audit & Testing: Periodic security reviews and vulnerability scans.
  • Patch Management: Infrastructure and dependencies are regularly patched.

Data Retention Policy

  • Coordinates / Solar requests: Processed in real time; not retained by default.
  • Project Data: Stored locally on device. If you opt into cloud backups, those backups are retained per your backup settings and our cloud provider policy.
  • Anonymous Analytics: Retained for 24 months, then deleted.
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging, then purged.

Deleting your data: Because we do not retain personal identifiers by default, there is no central personal profile to delete. Device project data may be removed via app settings, by clearing app data, or by uninstalling the app. If you opted into cloud backup, contact privacy@solarpartner.app to request deletion of backups.

4. User Rights & Regulatory Compliance

GDPR (EU)

Rights include access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. See how to exercise these rights below.

CCPA/CPRA (California)

Rights include the right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale. We do not sell personal information.

Global Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional consumer privacy rights. You may lodge complaints with supervisory authorities.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise rights or submit a privacy request:

  • Email: privacy@solarpartner.app
  • Response time: We will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours and generally resolve requests within 30 days (extensions permitted by law).
  • Verification: We may request limited information to verify identity before fulfilling a request.
  • No fee: We do not charge for standard requests.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 16 (EU baseline). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child provided data, contact privacy@solarpartner.app and we will delete it promptly.

International Transfers

If data is transferred outside the EEA, we implement GDPR-compliant safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or rely on an adequacy decision where applicable. Contact privacy@solarpartner.app for details.

Cookies & Local Storage

The app uses local storage for project data and small non-identifying tokens. Web deployments may use minimal cookies for session management. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced in-app and the "Last Updated" date will be revised. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.

Accessibility
